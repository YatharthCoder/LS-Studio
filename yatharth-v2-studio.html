The Studio

Games made

LS Studio — short for Lagging Stick — is a hobby game studio making browser games that are playable, experimental, and genuinely fun.

Everything runs in your browser using JavaScript, p5.js, and ml5.js. No game engines, no downloads — just code and creativity. From AI that learns to play Mario, to anything that pushes what a browser can do.
